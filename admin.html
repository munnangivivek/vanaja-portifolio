How to update your portfolio

1. Load Data: If you have an existing content.json file, click "Import JSON" at the top right to load your current data.

2. Edit: Use the sidebar to navigate between sections. Update the text, add new projects, or modify your experience.

3. Save: When you are done, click "Export JSON". This will download a new content.json file.

4. Deploy: Replace the old content.json file in your portfolio folder with the new downloaded one.
